      Hotel Booking – Frequently Asked Questions

      How do I book a hotel on your website?

      You can easily book a hotel by selecting your destination, check-in and check-out dates, and the number of guests. Once you find the hotel you like, click “More Details” and follow the booking steps.

      Can I cancel or modify my hotel reservation?

      Yes, most hotels allow free cancellation within a certain period. Check the cancellation policy on the hotel’s detail page before booking.

      Are taxes and fees included in the hotel price?

      All prices shown include applicable taxes and booking fees. You will see a breakdown of charges before confirming your reservation.

      Is breakfast included in the hotel price?

      Some hotels offer complimentary breakfast, while others may offer it at an extra cost. This information is displayed in the hotel’s details.

      What payment options are available?

      We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and in some cases, you can choose to pay directly at the hotel during check-in.

      How can I find family-friendly or luxury hotels?

      Use our filters to find hotels that are family-friendly, luxurious, or offer specific amenities like pools, kids' activities, or spa services.
