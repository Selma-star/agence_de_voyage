Constantine Algeria, Africa

Constantine, often called "The City of Bridges," is one of Algeria’s most breathtaking destinations. Built across deep gorges and connected by stunning suspension bridges, the city offers a perfect blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. From ancient Roman ruins to vibrant markets and cliffside architecture, Constantine promises an unforgettable journey through time and tradition.

Must-see attractions

Sidi M'Cid Bridge

The most iconic bridge of Constantine, suspended high above the Rhummel Gorge. It offers panoramic views and is a symbol of the city's architectural genius.

Palace of Ahmed Bey

A beautiful 19th-century palace with intricate mosaics, lush gardens, and a rich history that reflects Constantine’s Ottoman heritage.

Emir Abdelkader Mosque

One of the largest mosques in Africa, showcasing impressive Islamic architecture and serving as a cultural and spiritual center.
